## Surface cues
- **Midnight Ink Canvas** `#222222`: Primary page background and base-level dark content sections
- **Deep Graphite Surface** `#333333`: Elevated card backgrounds, slightly softer than the main canvas
- **Canvas White Light Panel** `#ffffff`: Distinct content sections that break the dark theme, offering high contrast and visual separation

## Component cues
- **Primary Pillar Button**: Main call to action button for high-impact interactions.
- **Ghost Pillar Button**: Alternative call to action, offering similar prominence to the Primary Pillar Button but with inverted colors for secondary intent.
- **Category Filter Button**: Small, contained button for filtering content or selecting categories.
- **Standard Input Field**: Interactive text input for search or data entry.
- **Full-Color Applet Card**: Prominent display block for showcasing automated 'Applets' with distinct brand colors.
