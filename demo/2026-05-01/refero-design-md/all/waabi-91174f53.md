## Surface cues
- **Canvas White** `#ffffff`: Base page background
- **Cloud Gray** `#e8e6e3`: Secondary background, primary card surfaces
- **Muted Stone** `#d1d5dc`: Tertiary card backgrounds, softer content panels
- **Midnight Graphite** `#191818`: High-contrast sections, dark cards, prominent backgrounds

## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: Main CTA for key actions
- **Ghost Button - Light Text**: Secondary action on dark backgrounds
- **Ghost Button - Dark Text**: Secondary action on light backgrounds
- **Link Button**: Tertiary action for navigation or emphasis
- **Neutral Card - Light**: Content container on light backgrounds
