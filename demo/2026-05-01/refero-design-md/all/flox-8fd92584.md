## Surface cues
- **Page Canvas** `#ffffff`: Base background for the entire page.
- **Subtle Surface** `#f3f3f3`: A slightly differentiated background for sections or light component backgrounds subtly set apart from the dominant canvas.
- **Elevated Card Surface** `#ffffff`: Used for cards and significant containers, appearing on top of the base canvas with distinct shadow.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Button**: Secondary actions, navigation links, and subtle interactive elements.
- **Outline Ghost Button**: Secondary navigation items, links with subtle visual weight.
- **Primary Action Button**: Main calls-to-action.
- **Gradient Call-to-Action Button**: Prominent, high-priority calls-to-action.
- **Elevated Card**: Content containers requiring visual emphasis or separation.
