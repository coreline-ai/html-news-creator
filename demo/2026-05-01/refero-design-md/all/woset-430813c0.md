## Component cues
- **Ghost Button**: Secondary actions, navigation links, filtering options.
- **Primary Action Button**: Main call-to-actions, prominent interactive elements.
- **Text Input**: Form fields for user data entry.
- **Full-Width Primary Button**: Key navigational or submission actions, often at the bottom of forms or sections requiring higher emphasis.
- **Product Card**: Displaying individual products with imagery and pricing.
