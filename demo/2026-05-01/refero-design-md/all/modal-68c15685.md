## Surface cues
- **Base Canvas** `#000000`: Primary page background, providing a deep, dark foundation for all content.
- **Navigation Surface** `#212525`: Background for the main navigation bar, slightly elevated visually from the base canvas.
- **Light Panel** `#def0dd`: Background for specific content sections that require a lighter theme or contrast, such as community testimonials.

## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: Main call to action
- **Ghost Button**: Secondary action with low visual impact
- **Subtle Ghost Button**: Tertiary action or contextual link
- **Minimal Link Button**: Text-only action within content
- **Section Card**: Content grouping within sections
