## Surface cues
- **Fog Canvas** `#eeeeee`: Primary page background for light-themed sections, offering a subtle, soft base.
- **Canvas White Panel** `#ffffff`: Elevated white panels or card-like structures on the Fog Canvas, providing contrast for content.
- **Midnight Ink Overlay** `#000000`: Dominant background for full-bleed dark sections and modal overlays, creating stark contrast and focus.
