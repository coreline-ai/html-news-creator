- Section Gap: `48-64px`

## Component cues
- **Primary Dark Button**: Call-to-action button for primary actions.
- **Outline Ghost Button**: Secondary call-to-action or navigational options.
- **Pill Ghost Button**: Small, less prominent actions or filters.
- **Default Card**: Content containers for features, data, or product previews.
- **Hero Card**: Prominent display cards for key product features or testimonials.
