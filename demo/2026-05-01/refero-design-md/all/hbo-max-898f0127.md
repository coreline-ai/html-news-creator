## Surface cues
- **Midnight Abyss** `#000000`: Base page canvas, content card background.
- **Deep Shadow** `#050409`: Slightly elevated surface, for contextual grouping like pricing plan containers or secondary content blocks.
- **Silver Moonlight** `#ffffff`: Prominent background for specific UI elements that require high contrast, such as pricing plan cards or primary sign-up buttons.

## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: Filled button for primary calls to action.
- **Ghost Action Button**: Outline button for secondary or tertiary actions, often in areas with dark backgrounds.
- **Text Link Button**: Minimal interactive element, typically used for navigation or in-line actions within text.
- **White Surface Card**: Used for content blocks requiring a lighter surface for contrast, such as pricing plans.
- **Dark Content Card**: Default card for content presentation, blending into the dark background.
