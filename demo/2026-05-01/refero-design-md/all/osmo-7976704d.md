## Surface cues
- **Arctic White Canvas** `#f4f4f4`: Primary page background, expansive light areas.
- **Concrete Gray Card** `#eaeaea`: Default card and input field background, providing a subtle elevation from the canvas.
- **Silver Mist Panel** `#e1e1e1`: Secondary light card background, list backgrounds.
- **Deep Charcoal Module** `#312e2`: Darker card backgrounds, prominent content blocks for contrast.
- **Ink Black Overlay** `#201d1d`: Deepest backgrounds, navigation bars, and interactive panel backgrounds.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Button - Dark**: Secondary action or navigation link.
- **Ghost Button - Light**: Secondary action or navigation link on dark backgrounds.
- **Pill Ghost Button - Light**: Specialized secondary action, often for filters or tags.
- **Unstyled Link Button**: Contextual actions or text links that behave like buttons but visually integrate into body text.
- **Default Card**: Content grouping, larger interactive elements.
