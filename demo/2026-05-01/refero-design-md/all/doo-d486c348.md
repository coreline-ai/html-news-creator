## Surface cues
- **Canvas White** `#ffffff`: Dominant page background and primary, elevated surfaces like modals or top-level cards.
- **Subtle Gray** `#f7f7f7`: Secondary background for cards, providing a soft contrast to the main canvas.
- **Accent Card White** `#e7efff`: A very light, tinted background used for specific cards to add subtle visual variation.

## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: Filled button
- **Ghost Navigation Link**: Primary navigation item, text-based
- **Default Content Card**: Standard container for information
- **Minimal Card**: Container without explicit padding
- **Accent Card**: Card with a light background tint
