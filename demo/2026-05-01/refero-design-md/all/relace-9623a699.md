## Typography direction

## Spacing / shape
- Section Gap: `40px`
- Card Padding: `20px`
- Element Gap: `8px`
- Radius: `icons: 800px, buttons: 800px, controls: 2px`

## Surface cues
- **Canvas Cream** `#fffef2`: Primary page background and base for most content.
- **Pale Stone** `#edece0`: Used for subtly differentiated content blocks and panels, offering a slight visual lift from the canvas.

## Component cues
- **Primary Filled Button**: Call to Action
- **Ghost Outline Button**: Secondary Action
- **Navigation Link**: Top Navigation Item
- **Main Heading**: Primary Content Title
- **Body Text**: Standard Paragraph Text
