## Spacing / shape
- Section Gap: `50px`
- Card Padding: `22px`
- Element Gap: `20px`
- Radius: `cards: 16px, links: 12px, inputs: 4px, buttons: 100px, largeElements: 38px`

## Surface cues
- **Midnight Canvas** `#000000`: Dominant page background, providing a deep, dark atmospheric base.
- **Slate Module** `#7d7d7d`: Secondary background for cards and ghost buttons, offering a slightly lighter, distinct surface within the dark theme.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Header Button**: Navigation and secondary actions
- **Pill Button**: Primary Call to Action
- **Feature Card**: Content container for features or case studies
- **Large Radius Card**: Prominent content blocks
- **Inline Text Link**: Linked text within content
