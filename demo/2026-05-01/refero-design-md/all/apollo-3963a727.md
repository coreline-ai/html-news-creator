## Surface cues
- **Canvas** `#f7f5f2`: Base page background and elevated neutral elements.
- **Primary Card Surface** `#ffffff`: Default card backgrounds, creating a subtle lift from the canvas.
- **Section Background** `#ccc9c6`: Distinct background for major content sections, navigation, and footer, providing clear visual segmentation.

## Component cues
- **Navigation Link**: Primary navigation links in header.
- **Primary Action Button**: Main call-to-action button.
- **Secondary Action Button**: Outline style button for less prominent actions.
- **Ghost Button**: Minimalist buttons for secondary or tertiary actions.
- **Social Sign-Up Button**: Buttons for third-party authentication.
