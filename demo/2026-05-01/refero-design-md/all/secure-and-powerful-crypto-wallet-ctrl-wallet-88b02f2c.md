## Surface cues
- **Light Ash Canvas** `#f9faf9`: Primary page background, base canvas.
- **Polar White Surface** `#ffffff`: Elevated content areas, input backgrounds.
- **Pebble Gray Card** `#ecefec`: Card backgrounds, slightly differentiated from the canvas.

## Component cues
- **Primary Filled Button**: Primary Call-to-action button, e.g., 'Download'.
- **Ghost Bordered Button**: Secondary action or navigation item.
- **Pill Bordered Button**: Outline button with extreme rounded corners, typically for secondary actions.
- **FAQ Accordion Card**: Content container for questions and answers, appearing as expandable sections.
- **Feature Highlight Card**: Prominent information display or feature showcase card.
