- **Night Canvas** `#0a0118`: Global page background and default section base

- **Overlay Veil** `#16092a`: Gradient fades and blurred overlays above the base canvas
- **Ultraviolet Glow** `#713dff`: Illuminated accent field beneath hero imagery and active focal points
