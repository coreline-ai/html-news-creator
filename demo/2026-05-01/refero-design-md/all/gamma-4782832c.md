## Surface cues
- **Deep Space** `#0c0c0d`: Primary page background, base canvas.
- **Carbon Surface** `#1d1d1`: Elevated card backgrounds, creating subtle separation from the base canvas.
- **Light Panel** `#ffffff`: Background for light-themed sections or prominent cards that stand out on the dark canvas.

## Component cues
- **Pill Button**: Flexible interface button
- **Link Button**: Text-based actions
- **CTA Button Large**: Primary action within a card or hero
- **Muted Text Button**: Subtle, secondary actions
- **Informational Card**: Container for content sections
