## Surface cues
- **Void Black Canvas** `#10020a`: Primary page background and base surface for most content blocks.
- **Twilight Raisin Card** `#1e0313`: Slightly elevated card backgrounds, often used for featured content or interactive panels.
- **Deep Plum Accent** `#360518`: Accent background for smaller, visually distinct cards or components.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Button**: Primary action button with high contrast text.
- **Default Data Card**: Informational display card within data heavy sections.
- **Prominent Feature Card**: Elevated highlight card for key features or content blocks.
- **Miniature Preview Card**: Small, functional card for previews or status updates.
- **Navigation Link**: Header and footer navigation elements.
