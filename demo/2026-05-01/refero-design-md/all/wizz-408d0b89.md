## Surface cues
- **Canvas Background** `#000000`: Dominant background for the entire application and sections.
- **Card Surface** `#000000`: Background for cards and information panels, often with an inset border for definition.
- **Subtle Surface** `#292929`: Used for ghost buttons and secondary background elements, providing a slight elevation from the canvas without strong contrast.
- **Input Background (Light)** `#eeeeee`: Background for input fields, typically on lighter content sections.

## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: The main call to action button.
- **Ghost Header Button**: Used for secondary actions in the header.
- **Nav Link**: Standard navigation item with minimal styling.
- **Cookie Consent Card**: Information panel for cookie settings.
- **Text Input (Dark)**: Standard text input field in a dark context.
