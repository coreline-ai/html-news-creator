## Surface cues
- **Obsidian Canvas** `#181818`: Base page background, deepest layer.
- **Dark Panel** `#1c1c1c`: Primary component surface, cards and larger content blocks.
- **Interactive Surface** `#171717`: Surface for elements with distinct interactive shadows or accents, often lighter than the base panel.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Header Button**: Navigation and secondary actions in the header.
- **Primary Filled Button**: Main calls to action.
- **Secondary Filled Button**: Less prominent actions, within darker components.
- **Shadowed Card**: Content containers with visual emphasis.
- **Plain Card**: Content containers without elevation.
