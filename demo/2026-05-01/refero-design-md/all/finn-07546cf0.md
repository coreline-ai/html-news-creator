## Surface cues
- **Canvas** `#ffffff`: Main page background and underlying content area.
- **Card Background** `#f9f9f9`: Primary background for cards and secondary content blocks.
- **Elevated Card / Modal** `#ffffff`: Used for specific cards that might appear over a Pale Gray background, or for modal/flyout elements.

## Component cues
- **Primary Filled Button**: Call to action for key actions.
- **Outlined Button**: Secondary actions that need less prominence.
- **Card, Pale Gray**: Container for product listings or feature blocks.
- **Card, Paper White**: General content container, often within a Pale Gray section.
- **Text Input**: Form fields for user input.
