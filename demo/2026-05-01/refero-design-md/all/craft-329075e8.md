## Surface cues
- **Parchment White** `#f7f5f2`: Dominant page and base canvas background.
- **Linen Mist** `#eae6df`: Secondary background for subtle content grouping or ghost elements.
- **Night Forest** `#1d3023`: Darker card backgrounds, hero sections, and visual content containers.

## Component cues
- **Ghost Text Button**: Navigation, inline actions, and secondary calls-to-action.
- **Primary Action Button**: Main calls-to-action.
- **Subtle Pill Button**: Tertiary navigation or filter elements.
- **Feature Card - Transparent**: To group related content in a flexible layout.
- **Content Block Card**: Informational cards or statistics.
