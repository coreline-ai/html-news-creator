## Component cues
- **Primary Call-to-Action Button**: High-priority action trigger
- **Ghost Navigation Button**: Secondary navigation and internal links within headers.
- **White Pill Button**: General secondary action or tag.
- **Whisper White Tag Button**: Informational tags or filter options.
- **Standard Card**: Content grouping, feature display, informational blocks.
