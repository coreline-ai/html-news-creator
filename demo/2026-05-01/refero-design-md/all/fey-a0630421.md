## Component cues
- **Primary Action Button**: Filled button indicating primary action.
- **Ghost Navigation Button**: Subtle, non-filled button for navigation or secondary actions.
- **Subtle Pill Button**: Discreet interactive elements or tags.

- **Elevated Tooltip Card**: Floating informational panels.
